# Founders actively commiserate about investor updates — word-of-mouth may be the distribution path

Both early interviewees mentioned communities of other founders or operators where
investor update pain is a shared topic. The problem is not silently suffered — it is
discussed. This suggests word-of-mouth distribution is plausible if the product solves
the problem visibly.

**Evidence:** 2/2 interviews mentioned peer commiseration unprompted. Quote: "We
commiserate about investor updates all the time." No specific communities or platforms
were named in the first two interviews — this needs follow-up.

**Implication for product:** Distribution may be word-of-mouth through founder
communities (e.g., YC Slack, Lenny's community, specific cohort groups). Prioritize
making early users want to tell other founders about it. Consider whether a public
"here's the update I generated" sharing feature could seed virality.

**Open question:** Which specific communities? Are there influential founders or VCs
who shape opinion in this space? Map this in outreach planning.
