# Focus on investor updates for seed-stage founders, not general business reporting

Narrow to the specific workflow of investor updates for seed-to-Series A founders,
rather than building a general recurring report automation tool.

**Made:** 2026-06-14

**Why we made this decision:** The general recurring report automation space is
crowded (Zapier, Supermetrics, Glean, dozens of BI tools) and undifferentiated. The
investor update workflow is a specific, high-stakes, high-frequency workflow with a
clear trust problem that existing tools do not solve. The founder has personal
experience with this exact workflow at two companies — that is founder-market fit
that does not exist for the general case.

**Alternatives considered:**
- *General recurring report automation* — Score: 8/15. Weak founder fit, crowded
  market, no defensible edge.
- *Board/leadership reporting for Series A+ finance leaders* — Score: 11/15. Stronger
  pain, but requires deep accounting domain knowledge and has an unclear buyer (finance
  leaders aren't usually the decision maker). More complex distribution.

**What would cause us to revisit this:** If customer discovery reveals that (a) the
investor update market is too small to build a business on, or (b) the pain is
equally strong for board reporting and the distribution is better there. Threshold:
3+ consecutive interviews where the interviewee says "investor updates aren't my
biggest pain, board prep is."
