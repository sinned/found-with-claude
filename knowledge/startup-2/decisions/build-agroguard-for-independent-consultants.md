# Build AgroGuard — a resistance-aware decision & memory layer — for independent crop consultants first, not farmers, co-ops, or hardware

**Made:** 2026-06-14
**Status:** active

The product is a software "resistance brain" for the agronomist: auto-ingests as-applied/field
data, holds a field-by-field resistance map, and warns before each pass when a program stacks
modes of action — with explainable, signable recommendations. Beachhead = the independent
consultant, the most aligned buyer who can purchase without committee. The farmer is the
beneficiary, not the buyer; the co-op is expansion (it has budget but a structural "sell less"
conflict and slow approval).

**Alternatives considered:**
- Direct-to-farmer app — rejected: farmers buy the trusted person, not software.
- Co-op enterprise as beachhead — deferred: budget exists but the product undercuts its
  chemical revenue and approval is slow; better as expansion once the wedge is proven.
- Logistics/route optimization for large operators — deferred: real pain, but different
  product and customer.
- Precision hardware / biologicals / genetics — rejected for now: capital- and
  expertise-intensive; we are the brain on top of existing precision tools.

**What would cause us to revisit this:** Buyer-discovery shows independents won't pay enough
to build a business (then test co-op enterprise or logistics); OR consultants won't trust an
explainable recommendation enough to sign it (then the core product thesis is wrong).
