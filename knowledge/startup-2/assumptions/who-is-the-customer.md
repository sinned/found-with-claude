# The customer is the farmer (operator economics) — not the agronomist or the retailer/buyer

**Validation status:** unvalidated
The founder thesis casts the farmer as the hero who saves money and grows more. But the
reduced-chemical space contains at least three distinct buyers with different motivations:
the **farmer** (input cost, yield), the **agronomist** (advice, trust, liability), and the
**retailer/buyer/regulator** (residue compliance). These are three different companies.
The residue/compliance opportunity quietly assumes the customer is *not* the farmer.

**What "validated" looks like:** Discovery confirms the farmer both feels the pain acutely
and controls the budget/decision for the proposed product.
**What "invalidated" looks like:** The real decision-maker or payer turns out to be the
agronomist or retailer, requiring a different product and GTM.
**How we'll test it:** In discovery, for each pain, explicitly map who feels it, who decides,
and who pays.
**Current evidence:** None — founder thesis assumes the farmer.
