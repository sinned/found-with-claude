talked to Jake today - he runs RevOps at a 200 person SaaS co. Basically said the whole
problem is that he has to pull the same 5 reports every Monday morning and paste them
into a doc to send to the CEO. Takes him 2 hours. He's done this for 3 years. When I 
asked why he doesn't automate it he said he tried with Zapier but the formatting was
always wrong and his CEO would send it back. "I spend more time fixing the automation
than I would just doing it manually."

also - he mentioned that 2 of his peers at other companies do the exact same thing.
They're all in a slack group together lol. That's an interesting signal.

---

Random thought: what if you could just tell Claude "here are my 5 data sources, 
produce my CEO update every Monday" and it would handle the formatting exactly 
the way you want it? Like a personal RevOps assistant. Is that too simple?

talked to maya - she's a first-time founder, raised a seed round last year. Said

can write a word." She said she would pay $200/month for something that just did this.

Key quote: "My investors only care about 5 numbers. But those 5 numbers live in 
4 different places and I have to manually reconcile them every week."

she said she knew 6 other founders who do the same thing. interesting. That's twice
someone has mentioned "other founders do this too."

Observation from my own experience: when I was at [previous company], we had a 
"board prep" process that took the finance team 2 days every quarter. 90% of it was
just... gathering data from different systems. Anyone who has been in a board prep 
process knows this feeling.
