# Customer Discovery Synthesis
*Generated: 2026-06-14 | Interviews analyzed: 7*

## TL;DR

Seven interviews with seed-stage founders confirm that data gathering is the primary
bottleneck in investor update workflows. The writing is valued and founders want to
keep it. The automation problem is real but trust is the core barrier — founders will
not send AI-gathered numbers to investors without verifying. The must-have product
feature is auditability: source attribution for every number.

---

## Patterns (appeared in 5+ interviews)

### Pattern 1: Gathering time > writing time
**Frequency:** 7/7 interviews
**Best quote:** "The writing I actually like. It forces me to think. The gathering is just tedious."

### Pattern 2: Format is stable
**Frequency:** 6/7 interviews
**Best quote:** "I've been using the same format for 18 months. My investors get annoyed if I change it."

### Pattern 3: Trust is the barrier
**Frequency:** 5/7 interviews
**Best quote:** "If it actually worked and I trusted the numbers..."

---

## Jobs to be done

- When sitting down to write my investor update, I want the data pre-gathered and attributed, so I can spend my time writing and thinking, not copy-pasting and checking.

---

## The riskiest assumption

Founders will send AI-gathered numbers to investors without manually verifying, IF
source attribution is shown inline.

**How to test:** Find 3 founders willing to try it with real data. Ask them: "Would
you send this to your investors without going back to check Stripe?"
