### 2. The problem
[2-3 sentences: who suffers, how much, what they do today.

### 3. The solution
[1-2 sentences: what you built. What it does, not how it works.
The key insight in one sentence.]

### 4. Traction
[Your best 1-2 numbers. Include context that makes the numbers meaningful.

### 5. The ask
[Amount + what you will build + milestone at end of round.
"$1.2M to build integrations for 10 tools → reach 500 active users by Q1"]
