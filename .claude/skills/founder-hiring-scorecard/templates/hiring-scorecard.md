# Hiring Scorecard Template

Fill this in before running /founder-hiring-scorecard.
The more specific you are about the actual job, the more useful the scorecard.

---

## Role: [Title]

**Company stage:** [Pre-seed / Seed / Series A — and headcount]

**The one sentence job description:**
[What this person does on day 1. Not the long-term vision — the actual thing
they will do this week.]

## Why we are hiring now

[What is blocked or missing without this person? Be specific.]

**Without this hire, we cannot:**
- [Specific capability gap 1]
- [Specific capability gap 2]

**This hire unlocks:**
- [Specific thing that becomes possible]

---

## The first 90 days

**Month 1 — what they will actually do:**
[Specific tasks, not abstract responsibilities]

**Month 2:**
[How the job evolves]

**Success at 90 days looks like:**
[Specific, observable outcome — not "integrated into the team" but "closed 3 deals"]

---

## What we are NOT asking this person to do

[Things that will NOT be in their job, to help you hire for the right scope]

---

## What we have tried already

**Have you looked at candidates yet?** Yes / No

**If yes — what made the strong ones strong? What made the weak ones weak?**
[This often reveals the real must-haves better than anything else]

---

## Team context

**Current team members:** [Brief description of who is already there]
**This person will work closely with:** [Who]
**This person will NOT have:** [Resources, support, or structure they might expect]

---

## Compensation range

**Equity:** [% range]
**Cash:** [$range]
**Note:** If you cannot name these, the scorecard will not help you — you will
lose candidates because you cannot give them an offer.
